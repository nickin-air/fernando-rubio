Sanguinaria

The first videogame I developed as part of a team. This was our project for the Masters Degree. The team was called Alea Games Studio, and consisted of 20 people divided in Development, Design, Art and Production. I was part of the Development deparment along with 4 other colleages.
My main role in this team was to develop the AI behavior for the enemies. Since the game was developed with Unreal Engine 5, I used the engine tools to program it.
Everything was done using mostly C++. The final result of my work included:

  • 3 different behavior trees and blackboards for three different types of enemy.
  • Use of AI Senses for detection.
  • Development of the enemies design (movement, attacks, area of interest...).
  • Use of EQS for the enemies movement
  • A Token Attack System for a more balanced experience.
  • Outside of AI I also helped with the final boss, the VFX implementation, UI and optimization and bug fixing.

The game is uploaded to steam and free to play.

About the game

Embark on a dark odyssey in 18th century colonial America. Control Aura, a vampire, and rescue your sister Lyra from a religious cult. Use your abilities and choose your attacks wisely in this 3D action zenith game in 3rd person.
You can use both close-range and long-distance attacks, dash through the enemy lines or dive in the blood of your enemies to avoid them. Absorb enough blood and you will enter in Frenzy state, boosting your speed and attacks for a period of time. Don't waste it!
